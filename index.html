About.

I don't just cut clips — I architect narratives that move people, break algorithms, and build brands.

It started during the quiet chaos of 2020. While the world paused, I picked up a timeline. What began as curiosity quickly became craft, then career. Five years, millions of views, and countless late-night renders later — here I am.

With a Data Science backbone and a creative heartbeat, I approach every project from two angles: what looks incredible and what actually performs. I don't guess what works — I know, because the data tells me.

From scroll-stopping reels that rack up millions of views to cinematic brand stories that redefine how audiences feel about a product — I make content that doesn't just exist in a feed, it dominates it.

Journey.

From curiosity to craft — the milestones that shaped my creative career.

2020

The Spark

COVID lockdown. A laptop. Endless curiosity. Started learning video editing out of pure interest — and never looked back.

2021

First Clients

After a year of relentless practice, started freelancing. Picked up clients, built relationships, and turned a hobby into a hustle.

2021 — 2023

Going Viral

Worked with organizations and societies to create content that hit millions of views. Grew pages, boosted engagement, and mastered the algorithm game.

2023

CapCut Developer Program

Selected for the CapCut Desktop Development Program. Spent 6 months testing, reporting bugs, and providing workflow improvement feedback directly to the dev team.

2024 — Present

AI-Enhanced Creator

Merged AI tools into my creative pipeline — HiggsField for scripting, Nano Banana & InVideo for visual generation, and AI-powered features in CapCut. Editing at the speed of thought.

Ongoing

Data Science × Creative

Pursuing Data Science alongside editing — bringing an analytical mindset to content creation. Not just making things look good, making them perform.
